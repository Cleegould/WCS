Welcome to Wayne Construction Services

We are a dedicated team with over 60 years of combined experience in the construction industry...

Casey Riffe, Owner

With an impressive 20-year career in excavation, Casey has honed his expertise through roles at industry giants like Kelchner and Bedrock Earthworks. His dedication to the field extends beyond his longevity; it's evident in his ability to tackle complex projects and lead teams to success. Casey's track record includes managing excavation projects that exceed expectations, underlining his commitment to excellence.

Randy Karn, General Superintendent

With over 35 years of expertise in construction leadership and quality control, Randall Karn brings a wealth of experience to every project. Over a decade of his career has been dedicated to superintendence, where he excels in project planning, risk analysis, budgeting, purchasing, and staff development.

Kimberly Riffe, Office Manager

With 22 years of experience at a top global financial firm, Kimberly excels as an Infrastructure Technical Manager. Her expertise includes strategic planning, budgeting, SDLC, and incident management, while adhering to financial industry standards. Kimberly's visionary leadership has been instrumental in shaping her organization's infrastructure, contributing significantly to its continued success.
