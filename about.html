Welcome to Wayne Construction Services

We are a dedicated team with over 60 years of combined experience in the construction industry...

Casey Riffe, Owner

With an impressive 20-year career in excavation, Casey has honed his expertise through roles at well known industry giants. His dedication to the field extends beyond his longevity; it's evident in his ability to tackle complex projects and lead teams to success. Casey's track record includes managing excavation projects that exceed expectations, underlining his commitment to excellence.

Randy Karn, General Superintendent

Bringing over 35 years of expertise in construction leadership and quality control, Randall Karn excels in project planning, risk analysis, budgeting, purchasing, and staff development. Over a decade of his career has been dedicated to superintendence, where he consistently achieves success in overseeing various projects.

Jace Fitzpatrick, Lead Operator

With over six years of hands-on experience in excavation and heavy equipment operation, Jace's expertise spans various heavy machinery, including excavators, skid steers, and bulldozers. Jace excels in overseeing and executing crucial aspects of construction projects, specializing in foundation work and demonstrating proficiency in handling footer, sanitary, water, and storm lines.
